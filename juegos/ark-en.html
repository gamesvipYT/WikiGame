ARK: Survival Evolved is an open-world survival game where players must explore an island filled with dinosaurs and prehistoric creatures. You can gather resources, build shelters, tame animals, and face various dangers to survive. The game offers both solo and multiplayer modes, allowing players to form tribes and share strategies.

What is ARK: Survival Evolved?

You are a survivor on an island called ARK, where you must avoid dying from hunger or cold.

To survive, you will need to hunt, gather, craft items, build shelters, and tame dinosaurs and other creatures.
